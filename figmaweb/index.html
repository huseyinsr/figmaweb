Why

Japan, an archipelago located in East Asia, is a country celebrated for its rich history, vibrant culture, and technological advancements. Consisting of four main islands—Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku—along with thousands of smaller islands, Japan is home to a diverse range of landscapes, from bustling urban centers to tranquil countryside and majestic mountains.
